QuantumPulse Technologies Achieves ISO 9001:2015 Certification

March 15, 2025
By Admin
Company News, Certifications
ISO 9001:2015 CERTIFIED

A Milestone in Quality Excellence

We are proud to announce that QuantumPulse Technologies Private Limited has been awarded the prestigious ISO 9001:2015 certification for our Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO), Engineering Design Services, and Proto Build operations. This achievement marks a significant milestone in our commitment to quality management and process excellence.

What This Certification Means

The ISO 9001:2015 certification is an internationally recognized standard that validates our quality management systems and processes. It demonstrates our ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements while enhancing customer satisfaction through effective quality management systems.

Scope of Certification

Our certification covers three key areas of our operations:

RPO Services

Recruitment Process Outsourcing with quality-assured talent acquisition processes

Engineering Design

PCB design, embedded systems, and engineering solutions

Proto Build

Prototype development and manufacturing services

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

This certification is not just an achievement but a commitment to continuous improvement. It reflects our dedication to:

  • Enhanced customer satisfaction through consistent quality delivery
  • Streamlined processes and operational efficiency
  • Risk-based thinking and proactive problem-solving
  • Continual improvement in all aspects of our operations
  • Strong leadership engagement and employee involvement

Certificate Details

Certificate Number: QP-ISO-9001-2025-001

Validity Period: March 7, 2025 - March 6, 2026

Certifying Body: International Standards Certification Ltd.

Scope: RPO Services, Engineering Design Services, Proto Build Operations
