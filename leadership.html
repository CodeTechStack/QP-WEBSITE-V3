Our Leadership

Executive Leadership

Mr. Mohan Ram

Director

Mr. Mohan Ram serves as the Chairman of QuantumPulse Technologies. With his strong technical background and visionary leadership, he has guided the company to achieve excellence in high-performance circuit design, product innovation, and quality engineering. His deep understanding of electronic design processes and commitment to technological advancement have positioned QuantumPulse Technologies as a trusted name in the PCB design and embedded systems industry. He has over 25 years of experience in organization. Under his leadership, QuantumPulse has grown into a trusted partner companies. Prior to founding QuantumPulse, he held senior leadership roles where he developed innovative approaches to talent acquisition and organizational development. Mr. Mohan Ram holds degree in engineering.

Mr. Raja Ram

Managing Director

Mr. Raja Ram leads major operations and service at QuantumPulse, focusing on excellence and client satisfaction. With over 20 years of experience in business operations, he has been instrumental in scaling the company's service delivery capabilities. He specializes in process optimization, quality assurance, and building high-performance teams that consistently exceed client expectations.

Mr. Eshwar Ram

Director

Mr. Eshwar Ram holds a degree in Engineering with a specialisation in Robotics and Automation, complemented by a master's from Warwick Business School. This combination of deep technical expertise and strong business acumen enables him to navigate both the engineering and strategic aspects of the electronics industry with ease. As a Director at QuantumPulse Technologies, Eshwar Ram plays a pivotal role in driving innovation and delivering end-to-end electronics design services. With a focus on quality, performance, and client-centric solutions, He works closely with teams and partners to bring complex product ideas to life from concept to final design. His leadership continues to shape the company's growth and its reputation as a trusted technology partner.

Mr. Sathya Narayanan

Director

Mr. Sathya Narayanan brings more than a decade of experience in strategic HR leadership and business operations. He leads key initiatives at QuantumPulse, focusing on aligning human capital strategies with business objectives to drive sustainable growth. Earlier in his career, he worked at Syrma Technology, developing expertise in employee relations, recruitment and human resources. At QuantumPulse, he focuses on operational strategy with a people-first approach, cultivating an inclusive culture that prioritizes respect and shared success.
