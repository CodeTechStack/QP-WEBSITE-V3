Core Facilities

Advanced R&D Labs

Multi-discipline labs for AI edge devices, embedded controllers and hardware-in-the-loop testing. Supports faculty-research, student internships, and collaborative projects with industry.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Vibration tables, tensile/compression testing, environmental ovens and electrical QA benches for prototype validation and reliability testing.

Prototype Workshop

Laser cutting, CNC, 3D scanning and multiple FDM/SLA printers to iterate enclosures, frames and mechanical parts rapidly.

Drone & Robotics Lab

Indoor and outdoor test zones with telemetry systems and guidance for UAV R&D, flight control and payload integration.

Renewable / PV Trainer

Solar PV bench, IV curve tracer and monitoring stack for MPPT research and IoT-based energy telemetry experiments.

Software & Simulation

Workstations with MATLAB/Simulink, CAD tools (SolidWorks, Fusion 360) and embedded toolchains for hardware-software co-design.
